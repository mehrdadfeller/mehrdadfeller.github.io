I created MoveSmart, a vehicle-sharing platform powered by BitLock connected hardware. It began with bike share and expanded to support e-bikes and other electric vehicles. The product included a fleet-management backend, user-facing mobile apps (iOS, Android), and access-control and telematics hardware. Several operators used it to manage their fleets in cities worldwide. We also developed a battery-swapping kiosk concept for e-bikes and e-vehicles for Toyota Innovation.
