I designed the first Bluetooth-enabled bicycle lock and took it from concept to mass production. Launched on Kickstarter in 2013, it raised $130K in 30 days from 1,000 backers. Over the next few years, several thousand BitLocks were manufactured in Taiwan and shipped worldwide.
