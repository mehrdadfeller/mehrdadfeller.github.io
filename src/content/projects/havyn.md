Havyn was a multi-family IoT platform that let delivery services access property gates and leave packages securely in appliance-like lock boxes. I joined as CTO and led end-to-end system design and implementation across IoT hardware, software, and the fleet-management backend. We completed the full system and closed a distribution deal with one of the largest residential management firms to host lockers nationwide. Ultimately, a lack of operating capital for scaling manufacturing forced us to wind the operation down.
