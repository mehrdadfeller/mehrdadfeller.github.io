I co-founded Picovoice, a startup training hyper-efficient edge AI models that run on resource-constrained devices. We began with voice AI — wake word detection, speech-to-intent, speech-to-text, text-to-speech, speaker diarization, speaker identification, and noise suppression — and later expanded to multi-modal vision and language models. Picovoice products have been used by hundreds of thousands of developers to date. During my tenure, I led technical marketing, benchmarking, product, and sales.
